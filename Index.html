💡 Energy Saving Tips

All Tips 🏠 Home 🏢 Office 🏭 Industrial

AI Recommendation — Based on current usage
Running your AC at 24°C instead of 20°C can reduce electricity consumption by up to 15%. Your HVAC accounts for the highest share of your monthly bill — this single change could save you ₹180/month.
💡
Turn off unused lights
Switch to LED bulbs and use motion sensors in less-used rooms.
🌡️
Optimize AC temperature
Set AC to 24°C. Every degree lower increases energy use by ~6%.
🔌
Unplug idle devices
Standby power can account for up to 10% of your electricity bill.
☀️
Use solar during peak hours
Run heavy appliances between 10 AM–2 PM to maximize solar offset.
📅
Schedule appliances smartly
Use timers for washing machines and dishwashers to run at off-peak hours.
🖥️
Enable PC sleep mode
Computers in sleep use 70% less power than when active.
🏢
Use natural lighting
Open blinds during the day to reduce artificial lighting needs.
⚙️
Optimize motor load schedules
Avoid running heavy machinery simultaneously to reduce peak demand charges.
🔧
Regular equipment maintenance
Well-maintained machines consume 10–15% less energy than poorly maintained ones.
